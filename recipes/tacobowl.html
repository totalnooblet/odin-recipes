Chicken Taco Bowl

Description

Delicious and low calorie taco bowl recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Black beans
  • Tomatoes with chiles
  • Tortilla chips
  • Taco sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Jalapenos
  • Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Bake chicken breast in oven
  2. Shred cooked chicken breast in pot
  3. Add tomatoes, beans, chiles, and jalapenos to pot
  4. Add taco seasoning to pot and stir
  5. Add water to pot and stir
  6. Bring to a boil then simmer til most water is drained
  7. Shred lettuce
  8. Assemble mixture with toppings and enjoy
