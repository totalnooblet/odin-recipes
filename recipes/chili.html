Chili

Description

Delicious chili recipe with beans and ground beef

Ingredients

  • Chili beans
  • Ground beef
  • Chili seasoning
  • Chili tomatoes
  • Green chiles
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet
  2. Combine meat and other ingredients in pot
  3. Simmer mixture until it thickens
  4. Assemble with toppings and enjoy
